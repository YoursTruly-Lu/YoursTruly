Powerful Tools for a People-Centered Internet

Toxicity. Click bait. Doomscrolling. These aren't just habits or human failings. They happen because the internet's built using algorithms that prioritize clicks, views, likes, and profit over human health and happiness. Millions of hours are spent each day on unproductive and mentally harmful content and thousands of teenagers suffer depression and anxiety as a result.

Yours Truly aims to change all of that. We're a fledgeling AI company with strong wings and a big vision: Absolute control of online experiences. We're putting people back at the center of the algorithms.

Web 4.0 - Building a Better Future

How long has it been since you doom scrolled? How long has it been since you stopped watching an otherwise good movie because it had too much content you just weren't willing to subject your mind to? Not super long? Us either.

In the past ten years, the internet has expanded beyond every techno-geek's wildest dreams. Worlds without number. Content without boundaries. Everything streaming twenty-four hours a day. It's amazing, but in the aftermath of the corona pandemic, we are swfitly acknowledging that it's also dangerous.

What this new fast paced internet world needs is filters. But not the dinky things it currently has, real-time, customized filters that will take given content, and soften it just enough to suit it to avoid your personal triggers. Stream recommendation filters that block ad-driven content and priorize media that helps you accomplish what you came online to do.

Web 2.0 was about [x]. Web 3.0 was about [y]. That's all fine, but we believe that Web 4.0 - the future of the internet - must return to the place it all started: Human feelings, human choices, and human outcomes.

At Yours Truly, we do everything we can to make sure you control the algorithms, instead of them controlling you. We are building dynamic, AI-driven tech that gives people what they came for - and nothing else.

Next-gen filtering.

The modern internet offers two choices: Watch or don't watch. Block harmful content, or embrace it. The first option creates a siloed internet, echo chamgers, and social media bubbles. The second is causing a mental health epidemic. Yours Truly uses cutting edge AI tech to offer a third option: Fine-grained, adaptive content filtering delivered in real-time. Spider phobia? No problem, ask your filter agent to convert all spiders into fluffy bunnies. Issues with language, religion, racist speech, personal triggers or political screeds? Configure your tools to summarize and detoxify harmful content without pretending it doesn't exist. Experience the internet, keep up withe the news, but on your own terms.

Flexible, Trust-based parental controls

Modern parents are overwhelmed, trying to manage separate approvals for every action your child takes on every app. It's exhausting, and unecessary. Yours Truly brings it all together by using an AI delegate to make decisions that align with each family's priorities. Requests receive an immediate response; parents are informed in case they want to make a change. And the best part? A single AI manages ALL the family's apps.

Social Media 4.0

Is Social Media about people, or about advertising? At Yours Truly, we think it should be about people, about bringing them together in ways that enhance productivity. About keeping them healthy.

Imagine a social media platform without pushy, ad-driven content. Without click bait. Without doomscrolling. Social media so safe, even children can use it. Yeah. We're building that.
